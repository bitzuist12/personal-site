---
title: "Why I Write"
date: "2026-04-03"
description: "On having thoughts and putting them somewhere."
tags: ["personal"]
---

I have thoughts.  Not groundbreaking, world-changing thoughts, just things that sit in my head after I read something, build something, or have a conversation that makes me think.

For a long time I kept them there.  In my head.  Sometimes I'd tell a friend, sometimes I'd forget.  Most of the time they'd just dissolve.

So this is me putting them somewhere.  Short pieces, mostly.  About history, culture, startups, whatever I'm thinking about.  Not trying to be an expert.  Just trying to think clearly and share what comes out of that.

That's it.  That's why I write.
